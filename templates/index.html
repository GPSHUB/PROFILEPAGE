About

Hi, I'm Jerry. 

I enjoy all aspects of electronics and electrical engineering. I have an MS in Electrical Engineering with emphasize in RF engineering. I have approximately 18 years in RF engineering and enjoy dabbling in and developing hardware on anything that is related to electronics. As a freelance engineer, I can help with new designs, troubleshoot existing designs, or provide initial consultation. No project is too small or too big, will give it a “go,” just contact me.
